Their lives depend on your donation

Following natural disasters like a hurricane or earthquake, many people rely on the generosity of strangers to help rebuild their lives.
Donations to disaster relief organizations, local food and diaper banks, and other charities can make a big difference to people in great need. However, we know that not everyone who can donate to these organizations ends up opening their wallets.
